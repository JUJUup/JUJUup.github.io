Who Am I

“Walking a road less traveled, where power trading, meteorology, and AI collide.”

I’m Haoxing Ju, an AI Engineer at LXE (澜序能源). I thrive on the friction between different ways of thinking — where quantitative models meet atmospheric chaos, where code meets the real world. This is why I chose this path: not to stay confined in lab scenarios, but to build things that actually touch the ground, that trade against the weather, that interact with reality through every line of code and every trained model.

(Also, PhD master out from Nanjing University in Jun 2026 — data assimilation and AI-based weather prediction, if you’re curious about the academic backstory.)
